Basic Controls

If you don't want to read the manual, here is a quick control list to get you up and running:

  • Select an aircraft by clicking on it with the left mouse button.
  • Turn the aircraft using the A and D keys or 4 and 6 keys.
  • Change the altitude using the W and S keys or 8 and 2 keys.
  • Change the speed using the Q and E keys or 7 and 9 keys.
  • Assign a waypoint after having selected an aircraft by clicking on "Assign Waypoint" on the sidebar, then click on the waypoint of your choice.
  • Create waypoints by toggling the waypoint button on the sidebar, and clicking anywhere on the map
